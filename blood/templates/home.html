Blood Components

Whole Blood

The most flexible type of donation. It can be transfused in its original form or used to help multiple people when separated into its specific components.

  • Used for trauma patients
  • General surgeries
  • All blood types needed

Platelets

Vital for blood clotting and essential for cancer patients undergoing chemotherapy or people with bleeding disorders.

  • Cancer treatments
  • Bleeding disorders
  • Organ transplants

Plasma

The liquid portion of blood that carries essential nutrients and proteins. Critical for patients with severe burns and clotting disorders.

  • Burn treatments
  • Shock patients
  • Immune deficiencies
