About Us

Lifeline Blood Bank is a trusted name in the community, providing safe and sufficient blood supplies to those in need. Our mission is to save lives through the power of voluntary blood donations, ensuring that no one has to wait for the blood they need.

We work tirelessly to connect donors with recipients, ensuring that every donation counts. Our state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated staff ensure that the blood we collect is of the highest quality and safety standards.

Our Services

Blood Donation

Join our life-saving community of donors. Your donation can make a difference and save lives.

Blood Storage

Our advanced storage facilities ensure that blood is available when it's needed most.

Blood Distribution

We distribute blood to hospitals and clinics across the region, ensuring timely and efficient delivery.

Donor Eligibility Assessment

We assess donors to ensure that all blood donations are safe for both donors and recipients.
